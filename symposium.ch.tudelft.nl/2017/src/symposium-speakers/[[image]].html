Information

On the 1st of May, our symposium about Cyber Security will take place. This symposium, organized by the symposium committee of W.I.S.V. 'Christiaan Huygens' will take place in Theater de Veste, Delft.

During the day, there will be two separate tracks and at the end of the day, we will finish with a discussion about the ethics behind cyber security, followed by drinks and the possibility to network and share thoughts on the discussed topics.

Location

The symposium will take place in Theater de Veste, which is located at the center of Delft. It is easily accessible by public transport, car and bike. It is a five-minute walk from Delft Central Station. There is a bus stop called "Zuidpoort" near the entrance, you can get there by taking bus 60, 62, 69, 80, 81 or 121.

The parking facility "Zuidpoort" is right next to the theatre. Please follow the signs to "Parking Zuidpoort".

For more information on the location see: www.theaterdeveste.nl
