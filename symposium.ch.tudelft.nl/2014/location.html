Location

The symposium will take place in Theater de Veste, which is located at the center of Delft. It is easily accessible by public transport, car and bike. It is a five-minute walk from Delft Central Station. There is a bus stop called "Zuidpoort" near the entrance, you can get there by taking bus 60, 62, 69, 80, 81 or 121.

The parking facility "Zuidpoort" is right next to the theater. Please follow the signs to "Parking Zuidpoort".

For more information on the location see: http://www.theaterdeveste.nl/.
