On the 29th of April, the symposium committee of W.I.S.V. 'Christiaan Huygens' will organize a symposium on Big Data themed: "Connecting the bits. Discover the Huge potential of Big Data". The symposium will take place in Theater de Veste in Delft and will be fully catered.

In the morning, the theoretical aspects of Big Data will be treated from an academic point of view. Whereas in the afternoon companies will discuss how they put this theory into practice. For more information about the program or the lectures, please head over to the appropriate section of the website.

The symposium will be concluded with drinks, where you will have the possibility to network and share thoughts with companies and other attendees.
