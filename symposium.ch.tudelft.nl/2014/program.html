Program

The program of the symposium is divided into two tracks. One of the tracks will dive into the analysis of data, whereas the other track will take a look at the exploration of data. After the opening lecture in the morning the theoretical aspects of Big Data will be treated from an academic point of view. In the afternoon companies will discuss how they put this theory into practice. For more information about the lectures and the background of the lecturers, please head over to the lecturers section of the website.

The program will be concluded with drinks, where it is possible to network and share thoughts with other attendees.

Please note that all lectures will be in English.
