Registration

In order to attend the symposium, registration is required. Unfortunatelly, all tickets are sold out and you are no longer able to register yourself for the symposium.
If you still wish to attend the symposium, please contact us at symposium@ch.tudelft.nl. If spots become available, we will notify you.

Furthermore, people may not show up on the symposium. Therefore we encourage you to stop by at the symposium in the morning. We may be able to register you on the day of the symposium after all.
