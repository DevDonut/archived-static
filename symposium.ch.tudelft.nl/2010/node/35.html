Location

The MustSee in Delft will be the place to be on April 27 for everyone who's interested in this symposium.

Address

MustSee Delft
Vesteplein 5
2611 WG Delft

Route description
The location is easy to reach by car or public transport. It is a five-minute walk from Delft Central Station. There is a busstop called "Zuidpoort" near the entrance, you can get there by taking bus 60, 62, 69, 80, 81 or 121.
Parking
The parking facility "Zuidpoort" is right below the MustSee. Just follow the signs to "Parking Zuidpoort". For more information on the location see: www.mustsee.nl/delft
