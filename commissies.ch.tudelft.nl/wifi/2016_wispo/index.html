Welcome to the web page of the WiFi! On this page, you will find alot of information about the upcoming skiing trip of W.I.S.V. 'Christiaan Huygens'.

Where and when will we go?

On the evening of Friday, January 29, we will leave the beautiful city of Delft to head for the even more beautiful French mountains. We'll gather with 50 enthusiastic skiers and snowboarders at 'Christiaan Huygens'. Overnight, we will travel by bus to France. When you wake up, you'll be at ski resort Le Grande Domaine in the lovely village of St. François Longchamps. The pistes here reach over 2500 meters above sea level and with more than 150 km of ski slope there is plenty of room for both beginners and the more experienced sporters. After a long day of intense activity or relaxing in the warm winter sun there will be time to have a drink in the famous Yeti Bar or one of the other small bars in the village. The party will continue till early in the morning. Luckily the fresh morning breeze in the mountains will help you wake up. This will continue throughout the week.  

Unfortunately, everything has an end (nur eine Bratwurst hat zwei!). On Friday, February 5, we will head back to Delft.

Costs

The trip will cost €339,00. This includes reservation costs, transport, accommodation, 7 days access to the ski slopes of Le Grand Domaine and a Yeti Gold Card (which provides you with cheaper drinks at the Yeti Bar). Material is excluded, but can be rented for prices starting at €59,00.
