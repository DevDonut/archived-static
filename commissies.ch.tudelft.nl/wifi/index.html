CH Rally 2016

To Belgium and Beyond!

Rally

During the Easter weekend, March 26 - 27, the WiFi will take you on a mesmerizing trip through the Netherlands, Belgium and Germany with the first ever WiFi Weekendrally. Solve cryptic puzzles to find your way to a variety of destinations.

Costs

The trip will cost €20,- per teammember. This includes a overnight stay in a hostel, lunch and a BBQ on Saturday and a lunch on Sunday. This has to be paid in full no later than March 18 at W.I.S.V. 'Christiaan Huygens'. You will need to provide your own car. Fuel expenses are not covered.

I want to participate!

Your team needs to consist of at least two and at most five team members. Please note that at least one person has to be a member of W.I.S.V. 'Christiaan Huygens'. In the event of too many teams, teams with only CH members take precedence. You can sign up your team for the rally by clicking the awesome car below! You'll be taken to the registration form, where you can fill in all the mandatory details.

W.I.S.V. ‘Christiaan Huygens’ (CH) is the study association for students of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at the Delft University of Technology.
